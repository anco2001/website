Brigadeiro Recipe

Why this?

Brigadeiro is an easy recipe to do and that takes just a few ingredients and much less time. Everyone loves chocolate so learn this recipe so you can impress your friends and family at any event or festivity.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of powered milk
  • 3 spoons of powered chocolate
  • 1 spoon of butter
  • 1 spoon of tea of sprinkled chocolate

Preparation

  1. Put the powered milk,the powered chocolate and the butter in a pan 🥛
  2. Mix the ingredients at low fire 🔥
  3. Withdraw them when they start to loosen from the bottom of the pan 🍳
  4. With the hands greased with butter, curl the mass and serve in paper forms 🧁
